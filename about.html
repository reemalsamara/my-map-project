About Me - Tamer Hosny

With a passion for real estate and a commitment to helping clients succeed, I've dedicated over 15 years to building strong relationships and delivering exceptional results. I began my journey in real estate after earning my certification in Ontario and have proudly represented clients across Mississauga, Toronto, and surrounding areas.

My Journey

Starting as a local property consultant, I quickly learned that real estate is not just about buying and selling homes — it's about guiding families, understanding emotions, and building dreams. Over the years, I’ve worked with first-time buyers, investors, and sellers from diverse backgrounds.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Honest advice, always.
  • Commitment: I go above and beyond to meet client goals.
  • Innovation: Using the latest tools and technology to market properties effectively.

Outside of Work

When I'm not working, I enjoy volunteering with community events, spending quality time with my family, and exploring new areas around the GTA. Real estate is my profession, but connecting with people is my passion.
